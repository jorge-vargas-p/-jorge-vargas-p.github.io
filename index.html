Kia ora Taiao!
(Hola World!)

My name is Jorge, I’m originally from Chile-Valparaiso but I have been living in Aotearoa for more than 10 years, 9 of those in Wellington and the rest between Auckland and Tauranga. About my values: gratitude and modesty, sometimes in this time where we are living, we don’t take a pause to say thanks or to appreciate what the person next to us, can do for us even if them failed in the way to help. These two values, I think stick and work perfectly together to show how much human we can be.

Why EDA? I have been working in the hospitality world for a long time, and I thought it is time for a change and find a new challenge for myself. And also, for my family - in terms of spending more time with them. Also, I like to create and build ideas from scratch.
