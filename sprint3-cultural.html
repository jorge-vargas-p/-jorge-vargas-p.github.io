EQ & IQ

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to identify, understand and manage emotions correctly, in a way that facilitates relationships with others, the achievement of goals and objectives, the management of stress or overcoming obstacles. Before any event that happens in your life, emotions, both positive and negative, will be there, and can help you and make you happy or sink into the most absolute pain, depending on how you are able to handle them. People with high emotional intelligence do not necessarily have fewer negative emotions, but when they appear, they know how to handle them better. They also have a greater capacity to identify them and know what they are feeling exactly and also a high capacity to identify what others feel. By identifying and understanding emotions better, they are able to use them to relate better to others (empathy), be more successful in their work and lead more satisfying lives.

Characteristics of people with high emotional intelligence

Identify one's emotions They are able to identify their emotions and know what they are feeling in each moment and why, and realize how they influence their behaviour and their thinking. Management of emotions. They are able to control their impulses, they are not easily carried away by emotional outbursts, they know how to calm themselves when their emotions are especially negative and intense and they know how to adapt to changing circumstances. Identify the emotions of others. They have a high capacity to understand the emotions, needs and concerns of others, know how to put themselves in the place of others and understand points of view different from their own. They have a high capacity to recognize the dynamics of a group, to relate to others, because they know how they feel and can act on it in the best way possible. For this reason, others are comfortable at their side, because they feel understood and respected. They also have a high capacity for maintaining good relationships, influencing others, communicating clearly, managing conflicts and working as a team. Thus, they often have a high social intelligence as well

How is it different to IQ?

Your IQ is what determines your competencies and individual capacities, but your EQ determines how you interact and treat people in your life, it shows how you are going to cope with pressures or face the crisis. The difference between these two is that no one can alter his/her intelligence or reasoning abilities, but anyone can learn to handle emotions, to bring success for him.

Why is it important?

The EQ is important because through it we are able to motivate ourselves, control our impulses, regulate our moods and empathize with others. It allows us not only to live with those around us, but to survive. It is, in short, the one that controls a large part of who we are.

"It is very important to understand that Emotional Intelligence is not the opposite of intelligence, it is not the triumph of the heart over the head, it is the intersection of both"
-David Caruso-
